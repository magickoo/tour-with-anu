About Me

Namaste! I'M Anuradha Agarwal

Hello and Namaste. I am Anuradha, in short Anu. I am a freelance guide licensed by the Dept. of Tourism, Govt. of India. I have been working as a guide in Agra for more than 7 years. In India, we have 27 world heritage sites, and Agra alone may boast of having 3 World Heritage Sites: Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Fatehpur Sikri.

I live in Agra, an ancient city that was the Mughal Capital and flourished in their time. I have an MBA in Tourism and Hospitality, and I love traveling. I would be pleased to share with you our art, culture, traditions, and my passion for this amazing country! With me, you will discover not only the palaces, forts, ancient monuments, and temples but also lesser-known stories and facts. I provide private tours for a more personalized experience.

I specialize in private tours for small groups, individuals, or families with children, and especially for those women who have curious questions about our culture, festivals, and marriage system. I love to answer them.

Don’t hesitate to contact me with your individual requirements to design your perfect Agra Tour. I would love for you to leave Agra appreciating its wonder as much as I do. I look forward to showing you Agra or the countryside.
